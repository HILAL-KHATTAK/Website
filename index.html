Intoduction

The World Health Organization (WHO, 2020) defines health as “a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity.”
Definitions of health have typically focused on the presence or lack of disease, illness, or injury. Wellness if defined as “the optimal state of health of individuals and groups” (WHO, 2020).
Wellness is typically thought of as reaching one’s fullest potential.
It is important to think about wellness as a concept of interrelated dimensions (which will be explained in detail in this module).
